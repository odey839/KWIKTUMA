Frequently Asked Questions

We have put together some commonly asked question

How do I book a delivery with KwikTuma?
You can book a delivery directly through our app or website. Just enter your pickup and delivery details,package,weight, and select your preferred delivery speed.
Do you offer same-day delivery in all cities?
YES! same-day delivery is available in select major cities. We're continuously expanding our coverge, so stay tuned for updates in your area.
How much do your service cost?
Pricing is based on the weight, distance and delivery speed. Use our built-in calculator to get an instant quote before booking.
Can i track my package in real time?
YES! every delivery comes with a unique tracking link, so you can monitor your package from pick-up to drop-off in real time, right from your phone or computer.
What happens if my package gets lost?
In the rare case of a lost package,we offer insurance-backed compensation and a dedicated support team to assist you immidiately.
Do you offer bulk delivery options?
Absolutely. We have specialized plans for businesses and bulk senders,including discounted rates and scheduled pickups.
